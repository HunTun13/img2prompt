Your privacy

Privacy Policy

This policy explains what is processed when you use Img2Prompt and the practical choices available to you.

Effective July 14, 2026

Information you provide

When you generate a prompt, you may provide an uploaded image or a public image URL, along with the output format and detail level you select. Img2Prompt does not require an account.

How images are processed

Your image and generation settings are sent to configured AI service providers for processing. A request may be routed through Vercel AI Gateway when a fallback provider is needed.

Img2Prompt has no user-account database and does not intentionally retain your uploaded image content after the request is completed. Hosting and AI providers may process or log request data under their own policies, security practices, and retention periods.

Use good judgment: do not upload confidential, highly sensitive, or private images that you would not want processed by a third-party AI service.

Image URLs

If you enter an image URL, Img2Prompt retrieves that image so it can be analyzed. The remote website hosting the image may receive ordinary request information, such as an IP address and request headers. Private, local, and unsupported destinations are blocked for security.

Security and service data

Cloudflare Turnstile helps prevent automated abuse and may process device, browser, and interaction signals. Vercel and other infrastructure providers may also record standard technical logs such as IP address, user agent, request time, and error information for security and reliability.

Analytics and cookies

Img2Prompt may use limited analytics to understand aggregate product usage and improve the service. If analytics is enabled, the site will avoid collecting image contents as analytics event data.

Sale of personal data

Img2Prompt does not sell your personal data. The service does not use uploaded images to create advertising profiles.

Your choices

  • Use a non-sensitive image and remove private metadata before uploading.
  • Avoid the URL option if you do not want the remote image host contacted.
  • Stop using the service at any time; no account deletion is required.

Children

Img2Prompt is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes and questions

We may update this policy as the service changes. The effective date above will be revised when a material update is published. For privacy questions, use the contact page.
